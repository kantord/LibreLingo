# Numbers and Math 3

### Numbers eleven to nineteen

*   The numbers 11 to 19 are variations of the word 10 (hamar)
*   Eleven is hamaika
*   Twelve to eighteen are hama (r removed) + the number 2 to 8.
    
    *   hamabi = hama + bi.
    
    
    
*   Nineteen is hemeretzi.

### Vocabulary Numbers and Math 3

*   hamaika: eleven
*   hamabi: twelve
*   hamahiru: thirteen
*   hamalau: fourteen
*   hamabost: fifteen
*   hamasei: sixteen
*   hamazazpi: seventeen
*   hemazortzi: eighteen
*   hemeretzi: nineteen
